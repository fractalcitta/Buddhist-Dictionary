  • uddhacca

'restlessness', belongs to the 10 fetters (samyojana), and to the 5 hindrances (nīvarana).

It is one of those 4 mental factors inseparably associated with all unwholesome consciousness (akusala-sādhārana).

Cf. Tab. II.
