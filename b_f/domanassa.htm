• domanassa

lit. 'sad-mindedness', grief,

i.e. mentally painful feeling (cetasika-vedanā), is one of the 5 feelings (vedanā) and one of the 22 faculties (indriya).

According to the Abhidhamma, grief is always associated with antipathy and grudge, and therefore karmically unwholesome (akusala) Cf. Tab. I. 30, 31.
