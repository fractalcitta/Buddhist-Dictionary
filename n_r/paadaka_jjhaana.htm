• pādaka-jjhāna

'foundation-forming absorption', is an absorption used as a foundation, or starting point, for the higher spiritual powers (abhiññā), or for insight (vipassanā), leading to the supermundane paths (s. ariya-puggala).

The foundation for the former is the 4th absorption; for insight, however, any absorption is suitable.

For details, s. samatha-vipassanā. - (App.).
