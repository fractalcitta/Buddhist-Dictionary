Buddhist Dictionary by NYANATILOKA

  • cetokhila

'mental obduracies', are 5 things which stiffen and hinder the mind from making right exertion, namely:

  • doubt about the Master,
  • about the Doctrine,
  • about the (holy) Brotherhood,
  • about the training,
  • and anger against one's fellow-monks.

For details s. A.V.206, A.X.14; D.33; M.16. Cf. prec.
