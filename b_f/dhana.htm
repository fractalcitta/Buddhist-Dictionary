Buddhist Dictionary by NYANATILOKA

  • dhana

'treasures', a term for the following 7 qualities:

  1. faith,

  2. morality,

  3. moral shame,

  4. moral dread,

  5. learning,

  6. liberality and

  7. wisdom. Cf. A. VII, 5, 6.

See 'Treasures of the Noble', by Soma Thera (BODHI LEAVES B. 27, BPS).
