Buddhist Dictionary by NYANATILOKA

  • manopadosika-deva

'the celestial beings corruptible by temper', are a class of devas (q.v.) of the sensuous sphere.

"They spend their time in becoming annoyed with one another, and getting into a temper, and thus by being bodily and mentally exhausted, they pass from that world" (D. 1; 24).
