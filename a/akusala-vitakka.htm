• akusala-vitakka

'unwholesome thoughts' 

as defined under akusala (q.v.). In M. 20, five methods of overcoming them are given: 

  • by changing the object, 

  • thinking of the evil results, 

  • paying no attention, 

  • analysing, 

  • suppressing.

Tr. in The Removal of Distracting Thoughts (WHEEL 21).
