Buddhist Dictionary by NYANATILOKA

  • viveka-sukha

'happiness of detachment', or aloofness (s. prec).

"Whoso is addicted to society and worldly bustle, he will not partake of the happiness of renunciation, detachment, peace and enlightenment" (A.VII.86).
