  • agati

the 4 'wrong paths' are: 

  • the path of greed (chanda), 
  • of hate, 
  • of delusion, 
  • of cowardice (bhaya). 

"One who is freed from evil impulses is no longer liable to take the wrong path of greed, etc.'' (A.IV.17; A.IX.7).
